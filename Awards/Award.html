Awards and recognition

Despite being relatively new to the market, Empower New Energy’s
unique business proposition and strong team of professionals have
already been recognised for innovative thinking and successful
project structuring.

The SolarQuarter Business Meet Ghana Awards 2023
ceremony, hailed as the foremost recognition platform
in the renewable energy sector, took place on
September 12th in Accra, Ghana.

Empower New Energy, known for its contributions to the
solar energy sector in Ghana, was honored with the
Solar Company of the Year: Developer(C&I) award for
our commitment towards driving commercial and
industrial solar energy projects across the nation. Our
dedication to expanding clean energy access and
promoting a sustainable future has placed us at the
forefront of the industry.

In 2020 Empower New Energy and its founder, Terje
Osmundsen, was recognized by the Africa Leadership
Awards. The event showcases the work of those who have
demonstrated commitment to the development of Africa,
contributing to positive social change and sustainable
development.

In 2018, Empower New Energy was selected as an
award-winner by the Luxembourg-initiated
“International Climate Finance Accelerator” (ICFA), a
public-private partnership set up to support “future
leaders of sustainable fund management”.

In 2020, Empower New Energy was awarded a grant
from the Sustainable Energy Fund for Africa (SEFA),
managed by the African Development Bank, in support
of its innovative approach to financing small and
medium sized renewable energy projects
